Daisy Louise Johnson

Daisy Johnson is a superhero with seismic (earthquake-producing) powers. She has the ability to generate powerful shockwaves and vibrations, which she can use both offensively and defensively. In the Marvel Comics, she is often associated with the superhero team known as the Avengers and the intelligence organization S.H.I.E.L.D. (Strategic Homeland Intervention, Enforcement, and Logistics Division). In the comics, Daisy has played various roles, including being a member of the Secret Warriors, a team of superhumans assembled by Nick Fury to combat threats to the world.

Powers

Vibration Manipulation: All existing matter and energy within the known universe vibrate at a specific resonant frequency on the quantum level. Forced into a state of Terrigenesis via her exposure to the Diviner's extraterrestrial contents, Daisy Johnson acquired the ability to sense the various frequency vibrations in her environment and manipulate them. Her powers are connected to her heart rate, and as a negative result she can lose control of her powers whenever her heartbeat increases during stressful situations.

Chloe Bennet

Chloé Wang, known professionally as Chloe Bennet, is an American actress, model and singer. She starred as Daisy Johnson / Quake in the ABC superhero drama series Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D. (2013–2020) and stars as Yi in the film Abominable (2019) and the television series Abominable and the Invisible City (2022–present).

From 2012 to 2013, she had a recurring supporting role in the ABC drama series Nashville as Hailey. In December 2012, she was cast as a series regular on the ABC series Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D., which premiered on September 24, 2013. Bennet portrayed a hacker known as Skye, who was revealed to be Daisy Johnson / Quake in the second season. Bennet was awarded the "Visionary Award" by East West Players, the longest-running professional theater of color in the United States, on April 21, 2017, at their annual gala. She dedicated her award "to all the little girls who want to be a superhero; I'm just a half Chinese girl from the southside of Chicago."
